About the Monsoon

The monsoon is a seasonal wind that brings life-giving rains to many parts of the world. It's a time of renewal, when nature awakens and the earth is refreshed. The monsoon season typically lasts from June to September, transforming landscapes into lush green paradises.

Heavy Rainfall

Monsoons bring 70-90% of annual rainfall in tropical regions, replenishing water sources and nourishing ecosystems.

Agricultural Boost

Essential for crops and maintaining ecological balance, supporting biodiversity and food production.

Monsoon Tips

Stay safe, healthy and enjoy the season to its fullest

Home Care

Regularly check for leaks and mold. Ensure proper drainage and use mosquito nets to prevent diseases.

Healthy Eating

Boost immunity with warm herbal teas, soups and seasonal fruits. Avoid raw street food during heavy rains.

Travel Safe

Drive slowly with headlights on, avoid waterlogged areas, and check weather alerts before traveling.
